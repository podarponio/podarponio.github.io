Privacy Policy

Podarpon takes your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. By using our website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect:
We may collect the following types of personal information from you when you use our website:
Information you provide to us when you register, such as your name, email address, and date of birth.
Information you provide to us when you create a profile, such as your education level, interests, and location.
Information you provide to us when you connect with other users on our website, such as messages you send to other users.
Information we collect automatically when you use our website, such as your IP address, browser type, and device type.
Information you provide to us when you take quizzes or assessments, such as your answers and scores.

Use of Your Information
We may use your personal information for the following purposes:
To provide you with the educational content and features of our website.
To communicate with you about our website, including updates and new features.
To personalize your experience on our website.
To improve our website and services.
To comply with legal requirements.

Sharing Your Information
We may share your personal information with the following types of third parties:
Service providers that help us operate our website and provide our services, such as hosting providers and payment processors.
Other users of our website, if you choose to connect with them.
Law enforcement or other government officials, if required by law.

Security of Your Information
We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, we cannot guarantee that your information will be completely secure.

Children's Privacy
Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Your Rights
You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information. You can do so by logging into your account or contacting us at support@podarpon.com.

Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting a notice on our website.

Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@podarpon.com.
